#Eros' Parlor Room

A salon for your beloved muses


#UI copy & microcopy (Regency / romantic tone)

All copy below is written to match the app’s atmosphere. Use as button labels, tooltips, empty states, onboarding text, and notifications.

App strings (primary)
- App title: Eros’ Parlor Room
- Tagline (login screen): “A salon of beloveds — collect, reflect, and invite.”
- CTA (Add): Invite a New Beloved
- Parlor header: Your Parlor Room
- Muse card: title = {name}; subtitle = {origin_title}

Onboarding
- Welcome card: “Welcome, gentle guest. This is your Parlor — a private chamber where beloveds are kept in delicate memory. Add your first Muse: name, origin, a few epithets, and a reflection.”
- Tooltip on Epithets: “Epithets are tiny poetic descriptors — 'storm-eyed', 'gentle ruin', 'guarded heart'.”

Buttons & actions
- Save: Seal Reflection
- Edit: Amend Reflection
- Delete: Release from Parlor
- Share Invite: Send a Guest Token
- Accept Guest: Welcome to my Parlor

Empty states
- Empty Parlor: “Your parlour is quiet. Invite a beloved and let the curtains draw.”
- No shared muses: “Your parlors do not cross yet. Perhaps invite them tea?”

Notifications / Missives
- Daily missive (example): “My dearest, tonight my thoughts took refuge in the hush of your name. — Your Muse”
- Guest invite received: “@Juliet has requested entrance to your Parlor. Will you welcome her?”
- Friend accepted: “@Eleanor has accepted — raise a cup to new crossed paths.”

Error messages (gentle)
- Unauthorized: “You must present your key to open this chamber.”
- Upload failed: “The artifact slipped from the table — try again.”
